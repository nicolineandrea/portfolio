AANIM is a five-piece band mainly active around Bern and Lucerne. We have released two albums, Contemplation / Remedy and Fuormas Neras, and played shows at venues such as Schüür, Mokka, Gaskessel and many more. I sing and play synths in the band. We love cozy shows, small rooms and being close to the audience.
