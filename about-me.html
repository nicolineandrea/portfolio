About Me

Hi, I’m Nicoline Andrea Janka, a musician, sound designer, artist and performer based in Lucerne, originally from Graubünden.

My work moves between composition, electronic music, sound design, game audio, code-based music, sound synthesis and performance. I’m currently completing my Master’s in Music and Digital Creation at HSLU, where I work with tools such as Python, Raspberry Pi, Max/MSP, Ableton and Reaper.

I perform with AANIM and kim.a as a singer and synth player. I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Music and Movement, teach singing and computer music, and work in cultural management for Sprungfeder and Werft, Das Probehaus. Alongside this, I’m involved in social media and video work within cultural and music contexts.
